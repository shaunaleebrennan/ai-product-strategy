# The Prototype Bet

## What I Built
A working prototype of Workvivo HQ — the AI-native headquarters for every employee. The demo shows a comms manager landing in HQ on Monday morning and moving through a single, connected AI experience that draws on signal from across the platform.

Key features built:

- **Ask HQ** — single AI front door for finding answers across the company knowledge layer, with sources cited on every response
- **Catch Me Up brief** — personalized morning summary of what happened across the org overnight, with confidence indicators and "why I'm showing you this" reasoning
- **Liquid Content** — the multi-format comms agent inside HQ, generating Article / Video / Podcast / 6 more channels with predicted engagement scores, recommended send times, and source-cited reasoning
- **Seer signal panel** — sentiment, flight-risk, and action recommendations surfaced as ambient context, not buried in analytics

## AI Value Archetype

Primary: **Copilot** — HQ is positioned as the daily AI companion for every employee, which makes Copilot the dominant archetype. This is also the head-to-head with Microsoft Cowork — our defence isn't a different archetype, it's better reach (frontline + desk) and stronger signal (Seer + cross-pillar data).

Supporting:
- **Creator** at HQ — Liquid Content, Compose, Pages, Journeys, Podcasts. Multi-format content generation, which Copilot structurally doesn't prioritize.
- **Oracle** at HQ and HQ+ — Ask HQ, Enterprise AI Search across M365 / ServiceNow / Workday, Seer Topic Grouping, AI Insights in Analytics. Sourced retrieval across the company knowledge layer — the legibility moat against Copilot's black-box answers.
- **Orchestrator** at HQ+ Enterprise — multi-step agentic workflows across connected systems. AI that acts, not just answers.
- **Automator** at HQ Core and HQ+ — Journeys triggered by hire date or survey completion, scheduled prompts, auto-moderation, post-publish engagement analysis. AI that runs in the background without prompting, where the value is consistency at scale, not creativity in the moment.

## The Bet in One Sentence
Microsoft Copilot is AI without a home — bolted onto apps half the workforce never opens, a disconnected experience that takes years to deliver value. Workvivo HQ is purpose-built for every employee, wherever they work — fast to value, delightful to use, and the only AI that sees how people feel and helps leaders act on it.

## Kill Criteria
in each kill case, we prioritise a move to deeper LMM extensibility - so Workvivo remains the epxerience layer; while deprioritsing spend on AI features that aren't landing well enought ot warrant the spend:
- Microsoft extends Copilot Cowork with a credible EX skill pack (recognition, sentiment, journeys, frontline workflows) and bundles it into M365 E5 inside 6-12 months — and customers default to it because IT already paid for it
- Workvivo's Cross-Domain Transfer loop fails to compound: engagement, sentiment, and content data don't visibly improve AI recommendation quality over time (the Loop 1 broken problem doesn't get fixed in M2/M4/M5 timeframe)
- Employees experience HQ as a publishing platform with AI features bolted on, not as a daily-active AI surface — meaning Catch Me Up, Ask, and Liquid Content don't earn enough daily usage to make the "every employee deserves a headquarters" claim defensible against Cowork's bundled distribution
